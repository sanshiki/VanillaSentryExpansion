# VanillaSentryExpansion — Introduction (English)

**VanillaSentryExpansion** is a Terraria tModLoader mod that expands sentry-summoner gameplay: more sentries, flag weapons, vanilla sentry changes, and accessories and armor.

---

## More Sentries

Adds a variety of sentry staffs and their sentries, improving the full-game experience for sentry summoners.

## Flag Weapons

Adds a summoner main-hand weapon on par with whips: the flag. You can left-click to swing and mark enemies like a whip, and use right-click to charge or plant the flag to recall sentries.

## Vanilla Sentry Overrides

Enhances vanilla sentries to varying degrees, adjusting stats or behavior while keeping the original style.

## Accessories & Armor

- **Accessories**: Sentry Anchor, cloaks, and more.
- **Armor**: Optimizations for the sentry-summoner branch, mainly for late-game armor sets.

## Other Features

- **Summon fatigue**: A debuff that reduces damage when the player tries to summon many sentries and minions at once, to keep the playstyle balanced.
- **Localization**: In-mod support for English (en-US) and Simplified Chinese (zh-Hans).
- **Multiplayer**: The mod supports multiplayer.
- **Calamity**: Initial compatibility with the Calamity mod.
